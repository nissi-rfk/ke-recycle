About Us

iRecycle is a government funded program where it has the mindset to make the world a better place.

The main focus is to reduce the impact plastic containers have on the world. For example, they

have taken the lives of innocent animals, released toxic gases which take away people's lives.

It is our responsibiltiy as humans to take care of our world in any way that we can.

The idea we members thought of are different recycling stations spread across the country, the user

would either use the app (available on the Android Store and the App Store) or go to the closest station
