Facts about Plastic Pollution

According to the National Geographic, plastic has affected the environment in different ways.

Here are some 4 facts about plastic production and pollution:

1. Half of all plastics ever manufactured have been made in the last 15 years.

2. From 2.3 million tons in 1950 to 448 million tons in 2015, production grew at an increasing rate.

By 2050, production is predicted to double.

3. Approximately 8 million tons of plastic garbage spills into the oceans each year from countries at the coast.

That's the same as dumping five garbage bags full of trash on every foot of coastline on the planet.

4.Plastics frequently contain additives that make them stronger, more flexible, and more durable.

However, many of these compounds have the potential to extend the life of goods if they are thrown away,

with some estimations ranging from 400 years to break down.

Our Main Target

As the name iRecycle suggests, our main target is to recycle the everyday items people use. For example: water bottles,

toothpaste, toothbrush etc. Infact, there are some statistics that show if everyone would recycle using the app iRecycle:

1.According to Aluminum Association estimates, if everyone recycled one aluminum can, 295 million new aluminum cans could be produced.

2.Furthermore, if everyone recycled just one can, it would decrease greenhouse gas emissions equivalent to removing 6,750 passenger cars

from the road and save enough energy to equal 80 thousand barrels of oil. and many more benefits iRecycle would bring to the country.
